Your Generosity Makes This Possible

We hope that you are as excited about our upcoming Food Swap as we are! As you know, coming to our food swaps is always free and you will never have to exchange cash for goods. Behind the scenes however, there are costs associated with organizing and hosting these meetings. Some venues charge for space, and supplies like name tags and paper plates can add up.

If you’d like to help support the Wilmington Food Swap, there are three ways you can do so.

Venue: If you own a restaurant, bar, community space or public venue and would like to host a meeting of the Wilmington Food Swap, please use the contact form below to reach out to us. We plan to meet quarterly so we are always on the lookout for where our next meetup can be.

Supplies: If you are able to commit to bringing supplies with you to a meet up, please use the contact form below to coordinate with us what we may need. Some venues are able to provide us with the supplies we need and others are not, so our needs are always changing.

A short sample list of items we may need include; folding tables/chairs, name-tags & markers, paper plates, paper bowls, plastic cups, disposable cutlery, tablecloths, napkins, cleaning supplies, beverages, and printed materials.

We ask that that you only volunteer to bring supplies if you are 100% sure you will be able to attend and bring said items. We appreciate your generosity, but if you are unable to follow though it can impact someone else’s experience negatively.

Money: If you would like to help and none of the above options are right for you, then you can still contribute by donating money to the cause. We will have a donations jar at the event available, or you can also connect to us via Venmo for an easy, digital transaction.

You can find us on Venmo as @Wilmington-FoodSwap.

Again, if you have any questions please use the form to contact us.

Thank you and have a wonderful day!
